Admissions


Registration for classes Nursery to Nine begins every year by the middle of January. Class XI (ISC) admissions start just after the declaration of X ICSE results.

First, the duly filled registration form along with required documents should be submitted to the school office. After the processing of registration form an entrance examination date shall be given to the candidate.

Second step is appearing for the entrance test. Admission in Nursery, KG is on the basis of an interview.

After the written examination an interview with parents and candidates is conducted.

After qualifying the entrance test and the and the interview the guardians are required to collect the Admission form from the college office. Completely filled in form supported by original Transfer certificate/Birth Certificate, previous class Mark sheet Blood Group Report should be submitted along with the fees for completing the admission procedure.
